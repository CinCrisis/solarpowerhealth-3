ABOUT SOLAR POWER HEALTH

Helping Others Feel Their Best Through Holistic Healing and Plant-Based Nutrition

Sandy Is a Nutrition Nut, Reiki Master, Crystal Healer, Hot Stone Masseuse, Herbalist, and More

If you haven’t met Sandy yet, the first thing you’ll notice about her is that she glows from the inside out. She is both unfailingly self-possessed and an unapologetic free spirit. Smiling is her default facial expression and she lives life with an unrestrained joy that radiates out from a presence of mind rooted in positivity and light.

Sandy credits her overabundance of good vibes to the lifestyle she actively chooses to live everyday. She strives to follow a clean, plant-based diet and has an insatiable appetite for sunlight, which she leans into for strength, vitality, and focus (hello, sunflower!). Raised in and around Calgary, Alberta, aka the Sunniest City in Canada, it only makes sense that she grew up to be a sun-seeking enthusiast.

Raindrop Therapy, Hot Stone Massage, Crystal Healing, Guided Meditation, Soundbaths, and More in Calgary, Cochrane, and Beyond

With Solar Power Health, Sandy aims to bring her passion for all things wellbeing to others. From relaxation treatments inspired by nature to personal guidance in food and lifestyle choices, Solar Power Health is all about living up to one’s full potential.

(Bonus: as a trained sommelier, Sandy’s also very capable of making excellent wine recommendations.)

Sandy is a Usui Reiki Master and Holistic Healer. For her relaxation services, she uses Raindrop Therapy (“I chose Raindrop Therapy as I believe nutrition is important from the inside out as well as the outside in!”) and VitaFlex Techniques. Offering Hot Stone Massage, Crystal Healing, Guided Meditation, and a number of other services using holistic methods, she will help you heal naturally and provide guidance for your own individual health and fitness journey. She loves to both study and share her knowledge on all things related to personal wellbeing, focusing on such topics as the chakras, herbal benefits, superfoods, and general plant-based nutrition. Sandy has been exploring the world of plant-based nutrition for 12 years (“Nutrition is my passion!”) and is certified as a Personal Trainer and Fitness Instructor through Canfit Pro.
